Privacy Policy for Not Another Dice Game
Last updated: 10/6/2025
Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how Not Another Dice Game handles your information.
DATA COLLECTION
Not Another Dice Game does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All game data (statistics, achievements, and settings) is stored locally on your device only.
WHAT WE DON'T DO
- We don't collect personal information
- We don't use analytics or tracking
- We don't share data with third parties
- We don't use advertising networks
- We don't require an account or login

LOCAL STORAGE
The app uses your device's local storage to save:
- Game statistics (games played, wins, rolls)
- Achievements you've unlocked
- Your preferences (sound, haptics, theme)

This data never leaves your device and can be deleted by resetting statistics in the app settings or by deleting the app.
CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
Our app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.
CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
